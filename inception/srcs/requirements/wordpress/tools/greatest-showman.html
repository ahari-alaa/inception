The Story

Born of Dreams,
Built on Wonder

Directed by Michael Gracey, The Greatest Showman follows P.T. Barnum — a dreamer who built an empire from nothing but sheer imagination. The film weaves together themes of ambition, belonging, and the courage to be unapologetically yourself. With an electrifying original soundtrack by Benj Pasek and Justin Paul, every song is an anthem.
